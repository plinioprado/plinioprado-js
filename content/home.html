Finance Projects

Financial controls - CTR series

Series of public Proof of concept applications experimenting technologies that can be used in Finance control and reporting. Some of these concepts and technologies were used in actual solutions delivered for clients in the past, other are POCs for future use.

They can be found in public repos whose name start with "ctr" in www.https://github.com/plinioprado

The front-end projects spread from older Angular 1 and 2, and some plain javascript, to more recent ones using ReactJs.

There are back-ends in PHP, NoteJs/Express and Jave/Spring, using either SQL and NoSQL databases

  • Finance control backend and REST API in PHP/MySql
  • Invoice simulator in MEAN stack
  • Simulator of pre fixed loans in Angular 2

"Brazilian invoice simulator in MEAN stack" was forked into its ctr-server-node serving its ctr-client-angular and ctr-client-react under development to test and add what was learned at Red Academy
